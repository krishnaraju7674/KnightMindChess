# KnightMind Chess Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

KnightMind Chess is a chess training app for Android. The current version works offline and stores gameplay progress locally on your device.

## Data Stored On Your Device

The app may store the following information locally using device app storage:

- Player name entered by you
- App theme preference
- Local chess rating and game statistics
- Puzzle score and solved puzzle progress
- Offline friend request preview data
- Offline invitations and friends preview data

## Data Collection And Sharing

The current version does not upload your data to our servers and does not share your data with third parties.

The current version does not use advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, location access, contacts access, camera access, microphone access, or payment features.

## Online Features

Some social and leaderboard features in the current version are offline previews. Future online matchmaking, cloud leaderboard, login, or account features may require collection of account data. If those features are added, this privacy policy and Google Play Data Safety answers must be updated before release.

## Children

KnightMind Chess is a general chess training app. Do not add chat, public profile sharing, or user-generated content features without adding stronger safety, moderation, and privacy controls.

## Data Deletion

Because the current version stores data locally on your device, you can delete stored data by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app. The in-app Reset Stats button clears local stats and puzzle progress.

## Contact

For privacy questions, contact the app developer.
