# Play Store Compliance Checklist For KnightMind Chess

Use this before every Play Store upload.

## Intellectual Property

- App name is original: KnightMind Chess.
- Android package is original: `com.knightmind.chess`.
- Do not use Chess.com, Lichess, FIDE, or other third-party logos.
- Do not use screenshots from other apps or websites.
- Do not copy chess puzzle collections from books, courses, websites, or apps.
- Do not copy another app's board design, icons, piece artwork, sounds, animations, or store listing text.
- If you use any third-party asset, save its license and attribution proof.

## Impersonation

- Do not claim or imply KnightMind Chess is official, endorsed, sponsored, or connected to another chess brand.
- Store listing must not say "Chess.com clone", "official", or "by Chess.com".
- Screenshots must only show your app.

## Privacy And Data Safety

Current version:

- Stores data locally on the device.
- Does not upload user data to a server.
- Does not share user data with third parties.
- Does not use ads.
- Does not use analytics.
- Does not request location, contacts, camera, microphone, or files.
- Does not have real chat or real online social networking.

Google Play Data Safety suggestion for current version:

- Data collected: No, if data stays only on device and is not transmitted off device.
- Data shared: No.
- Security practices: No server transfer in current version.
- Account deletion URL: Not required for current no-account version, but update this if login is added.

## Future Online Features Warning

Before adding real login, online matchmaking, public leaderboards, chat, friend sync, analytics, ads, purchases, or cloud database:

- Update privacy policy.
- Update Google Play Data Safety form.
- Add account deletion process if accounts exist.
- Add block/report/moderation tools if user-generated content or chat exists.
- Add server security rules.

## Store Listing Safe Wording

Safe short description:
Learn chess with AI games, puzzles, local rating, and beginner-friendly training tools.

Safe full description:
KnightMind Chess is an independent chess training app for players who want to improve step by step. Practice timed games against AI, solve tactical puzzles, track your local rating, view an offline leaderboard preview, manage a profile, and learn with beginner-friendly controls.

Required disclaimer:
KnightMind Chess is an independent app and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any other chess platform, company, or federation.
