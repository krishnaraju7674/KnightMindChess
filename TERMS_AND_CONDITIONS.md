Last updated: May 3, 2026

These draft terms are prepared for the first offline release of KnightMind Chess. Review them before publishing.

KnightMind Chess is a chess training and practice app. It includes offline chess games, puzzles, local rating, profile stats, leaderboard preview, and social preview features.

## Chess Rules And Puzzles

Chess rules are implemented for gameplay and training. Puzzle positions included in the app should be original, public-domain, or properly licensed. Do not copy puzzle sets from paid books, websites, apps, or courses without permission.

## Offline Data

The first release stores profile, rating, puzzle, friend preview, and theme data locally on the device. It does not provide a real online account system.

## Future Online Features

If online matchmaking, cloud leaderboard, chat, public profiles, or sign-in are added later, the privacy policy, data safety declaration, moderation approach, and user reporting/blocking features must be updated before release.
